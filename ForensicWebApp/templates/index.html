Welcome to Cyber Forensics

Cyber forensics is the practice of collecting, analyzing, and preserving digital evidence to investigate and prevent cybercrimes. Our tools and techniques help uncover hidden data, analyze suspicious activities, and generate detailed forensic reports.

Autopsy

Autopsy is a digital forensics platform used to analyze disk images and recover deleted files. It provides a graphical interface for forensic investigators to examine file systems, emails, and other digital artifacts.

Cyber Forensics

Cyber forensics involves the investigation of digital devices and networks to uncover evidence of cybercrimes. It includes data recovery, malware analysis, and network forensics.
